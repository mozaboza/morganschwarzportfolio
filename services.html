Available Services

Requesting a Quote

Pricing will vary signifigantly from project to project, so a free 15 minute consultation is provided if you wish to discuss details and receieve a preliminary quote. To schedule a meeting or request a service, please fill out the contact form.

Illustration

Illustrative services include full scenes, characters, miscellaneous posters, and logos. Pricing will depend on complexity, but a logo will typically cost around $50-$150, scenes and characters $200-$500, and posters will be around $150-$300. These are only estimates, so feel free to reach out with more specifics!

Layout

My layout services include page organization for magazines and books, advertisement layout, copy writing, and label making. Pricing for this category will range between $50-$700, depending on the size of the project or the amount of body copy being written.

Photo Work

Photo work refers to any photo editing or photography. Any photo manipulation will cost about $100-$400 and photography services will range from $200-$500. For photography services, please provide number of subjects, location and dates in the details prompt in the
contact form.
